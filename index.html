About Me

A highly motivated engineering student currently undertaking a combined Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) and Master of Engineering at The University of Queensland, specialising in Mechatronics Engineering, with experience in Aerospace Engineering. My skill set spans communication, hardware tools from the Arduino Uno to MIG Welding, and programming languages such as C, C++, and Python. In my professional journey, I've excelled in roles at XL Metal Industries Pty Ltd and The Atrium - The University of Queensland, showcasing proficiency in forklift operation, workplace safety, and delivering outstanding customer service.

International experience at Park Inn by Radisson London Heathrow Hotel honed my communication skills and adaptability in a dynamic setting. Actively involved in professional organisations and holding certifications such as a White Card for construction safety, I am committed to continuous improvement. I've demonstrated research and project management prowess through participation in the UQ Winter Research Program 2023, conducting influential research on comminution techniques, and leading a team in designing and manufacturing an Unmanned Firefighting Vehicle for the ENGG1100 project 2022. My dedication to excellence is reflected in personal projects, including the design and manufacture of a replacement ball joint and mount for a residential security camera using 3D modelling and printing.

Projects

Design and manufacture a ball joint and mount for a Residential Security Camera

Due to wear and tear, the ball joint attachment and the screw mount on an Anker eufyCam 2C Pro Wireless Home Security Add-on Camera failed. Through Fusion 360 and a Fused Deposition Modelling (FDM) 3D printer, a replacement was manufactured.
